Greens

Again, greens are materials which are mainly green. Here's a list of greens that you can use in your compost.

  • Scraps from vegetables/fruits
    • You can use peels of most fruits or vegetables in your compost. At the end, I'll mention which fruits and vegetables you cannot use. Leftovers can also be used. 
  • Grass
    • When you mow your lawn, any grass clippings that you use can be composted. 
  • Coffee Grounds
  • Plant/Bush Cuttings
    • When you trim your plants, any cuttings that you use can be composted. 
  • Eggshells
  • Any animal manure, except dog/cat manure
    • Note that this should be composted in small quantites every 3-4 days.
